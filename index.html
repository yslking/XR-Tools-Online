XR FOV Test ONLINE

This WebXR page tests the FOV(field of view) for XR devices ONLINE.

  • Click the ENTER VR button to start.
  • Rotate your head. Pull the trigger on your controller at the moment that the red line right disappears on one side of your view.
  • Rotate your head in the opposite direction. Pull the trigger again when the red line right disappears on the other side of your view.
  • The FOV will be displayed on the center of your view.
